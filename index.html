Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MediCare

How do I book an appointment? ▼
Simply register or log in, go to "Book Appointment", select your preferred doctor, choose a date and time, and confirm. You'll receive an email confirmation instantly.
Is my medical data secure? ▼
Absolutely. MediCare uses enterprise-grade encryption and is fully HIPAA-compliant. Your medical information is never shared without your explicit consent.
How does the AI recommendation work? ▼
You describe your symptoms in plain language. Our AI analyzes them and suggests the most suitable type of specialist and lists matching doctors from our network — ranked by experience and relevance.
Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment? ▼
Yes! You can cancel any upcoming appointment from your Appointments page. To reschedule, simply cancel and book a new time slot with the same doctor.
Is MediCare free to use? ▼
Creating an account and using the platform is free. You pay only for the actual consultations, which are billed by the individual healthcare providers.
